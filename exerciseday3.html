Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Carnaberries and,Bries

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions,tart cannaberries, and ceramy brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons oil, or for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrap rind of with a paring knifed if desired , though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen carnaberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachement, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. you don't need to actually knead the dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
